Artist Statement

Stellium is a ceramic series exploring the next evolutionary step of the human species.

Emerging from the accumulated weight of millennia of human experience, every emotion felt, every lesson lived, Homo Spiritus is the species that comes after us. Colloquially known as Raves, these beings carry the cosmos on their skin and wear their inner world openly, with no separation between what they feel and what they are.

Each of the five figures in Stellium is a distinct individual of the same species, unified by a shared origin and endlessly varied in expression. Together they form a penta: one is all.

To our present eyes, they shimmer with something we can only call cosmic. But they are not aliens. They are us, fully integrated, emotionally alive, and at home in the universe in a way we are only beginning to imagine.

Hopeful, because these beings came from us.
Humbling, because we are not the final form.
Curious, because they are so close you can almost touch them.
